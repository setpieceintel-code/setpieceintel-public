What we do

Transfer intelligence, structured.

REAL-TIME SIGNAL
AI monitors trusted journalist accounts across Telegram, Bluesky, and X. Every post parsed into structured intelligence within minutes of publication.
CONFIDENCE SCORING
Every transfer link scored by source tier, corroboration count, and historical accuracy. Know the difference between a tabloid punt and a Romano exclusive.
DEAL LIFECYCLE
Track every rumour from first link to signed deal. See how a story evolved, which outlets led, and when confidence inflected — a complete deal timeline.
SOURCE ATTRIBUTION
Every data point linked to its origin. Romano, Ornstein, Jacobs, Moretto — know exactly who reported what and when. Intelligence you can trace.
SQUAD INTELLIGENCE
Club-level position gap analysis. See which roles each club is targeting, budget allocation signals, and how incoming business maps to outgoing exits.
B2B DATA API
Structured transfer data delivered via API to clubs, agents, betting operators, and sports media. Custom league coverage, SLA-backed, built for professional use.
